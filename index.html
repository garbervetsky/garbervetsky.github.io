about

I am an Associate Professor at the Computer Science Department, School of Sciences, University of Buenos Aires, and a Researcher at ICC / CONICET. I currently serve as Director of the Institute of Research in Computer Science (ICC).

My work centers on static analysis aimed at Java-like programs and smart contracts, automated program verification, and program understanding and validation. I am also interested in technology transfer, having collaborated on projects with Microsoft, GitHub, OpenZeppelin, Medallia and other companies and public institutions.

research interests

  • 01Program understanding. Recovering models and behavioral abstractions that make complex software intelligible.
  • 02Testing & verification of rich-protocol programs. Reasoning about components that expose stateful, protocol-driven interfaces.
  • 03Static analysis for verification. Sound analyses targeting verification of Java-like programs.
  • 04Symbolic resource analysis. Inferring gas consumption, dynamic memory and energy use automatically.
